### Goal  

---

This project is designed to practice and explore skills and applications in quantitative finance, with a primary focus on the equity market. 
It emphasizes strategies suitable for low-frequency trading,aiming to minimize transaction costs while maximizing effectiveness.
The tools and models developed here serve as a foundation for deeper understanding and experimentation in quantitative equity investment approaches.
